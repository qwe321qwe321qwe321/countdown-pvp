Move: WASD / arrows  ·  Holding the bomb: mouse drags your arms — the bomb follows your hands (max reach = BombArmReach). Dodge red −5s shots, catch green +10s repairs. Space = shield. Not holding: mouse aims — left click fires −5s, right click fires +10s repair. Touch the holder to take the bomb. Projectiles vanish on any blocking object (walls, player bodies, bomb) — only a bomb hit applies the time effect. When the timer hits 0 the holder dies: coins and cards are wiped and they spectate until the next round.
